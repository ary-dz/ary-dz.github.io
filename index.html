Who Am I?

Hello! I'm Aryaman Dwivedi (my friends call me Ary),
an undergraduate in Computer Science studying at the University of Illinois Urbana Champaign.

Hailing from India, my passion for computer science was ignited during my childhood,
and it has been a driving force in my life ever since. I find immense joy in creating
innovative solutions through technology.

Currently, I am focused on exploring my interests in embedded programming,
while still maintaining my enthusiasm for web app development. I am thrilled

my knowledge and creativity to contribute to this field.

I aspire to continue growing as a computer scientist and make a positive
impact through technology.
